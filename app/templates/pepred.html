🫀 PEPRED — Preeclampsia Risk Prediction

Risks can be derived from maternal history and any combinations of biomarkers.
Useful markers at 11–14 weeks are mean arterial pressure (MAP), uterine artery PI (UTPI) and serum PLGF (or PAPP-A when PLGF is not available).

📋 See example runs →
Any field left empty will be replaced by a default value. If you do not fill the Test Date field, the risk will be based only on observations before the pregnancy (maternal history). Two risk factors can be computed: the total risk for PE (All PE) or the risk for PE leading to early preterm birth (before week 37).
