About


This platform is developed at the YOLO Lab as a central hub for translating academic research into accessible clinical tools. Our goal is to bridge the gap between data science and everyday medical practice by making research-grade models available through a single, easy-to-use interface.


Each tool hosted here is built on peer-reviewed research and real clinical data, designed in collaboration with medical professionals. The platform is continuously expanding as new studies are completed and new tools are validated, providing clinicians with up-to-date, evidence-based resources in one place.
