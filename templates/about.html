About


Developed by Tamar Michelson and Yuli Tshuva at the YOLO Lab, this platform provides physicians with a growing suite of data-driven prediction models and clinical calculators for pregnancy management.


Designed to support medical professionals throughout all trimesters, our tools utilize key clinical parameters to assess pregnancy risks, track fetal biometry, and identify potential complications. By integrating evidence-based predictive analytics, the platform aids in making informed decisions regarding patient monitoring, early interventions, and overall care strategies.


Important Medical Disclaimer

It is imperative to verify all calculations before use. The results generated by these tools cannot and should not replace professional clinical judgment.
